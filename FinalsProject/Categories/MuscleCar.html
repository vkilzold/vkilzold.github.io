Ford Mustang Boss 429 (1969)

The Boss 429 carried a seriously aggressive appearance, which few could overlook. Perhaps most noticeable was the Boss 429’s fully functional hood scoop, which was far more pronounced than that found on any other Mustang of the era. In fact, the Boss 429’s air scoop is still the largest used on any production Mustang to date.

Chevrolet Impala SS (1967)

This vehicle is finished in the beautiful color combination of a Madeira Maroon exterior over an Olympic Gold vinyl interior. Powered by a 327ci V8 which sends the power to the rear wheels through a 4-speed manual transmission. This vehicle was ordered from the factory with air conditioning and a tilt steering wheel.
